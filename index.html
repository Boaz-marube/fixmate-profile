OUR JOURNEY: From Idea To Impact

FixMate began with a flooded apartment, failed handyman calls, and a midnight crisis that exposed Nairobi’s broken repair system. In 2025, co-founders Boaz Marube and Brian Mwangi turned frustration into purpose—building a platform to restore trust in Africa’s informal trades. From coding during blackouts to onboarding vetted pros, FixMate has resolved over 6,491 urban emergencies, ensured fair pay for handymen, and brought peace of mind to countless renters. More than an app, it’s a movement proving that in cities where pipes burst and lights flicker, reliable help can be just a tap away. Think of it as Uber, but for repair services.

FAQs

FixMate 101: Everything You Need to Know.

How Fast Can I Get Help?

FixMate’s FixNow service connects you to vetted pros in under 30 minutes for emergencies like burst pipes or power outages. Non-urgent bookings are scheduled at your convenience.

Are FixMate Pros Really Trustworthy?

Yes! Every handyman undergoes 3-step verification:
-ID & license checks
-Skills testing (e.g., live wiring repairs)
-Background reviews from previous customers.
That ensures trustworthness and transparency in carring out their duties.

What If I’m Not Happy With the Repair?

With FixProtect, all jobs come with a 7-day warranty. If the issue recurs, we’ll send another pro—free of charge.

Can I Try Fixing It Myself First?

Absolutely! FixTips offers free step-by-step guides (with videos!) for minor repairs like unclogging drains or resetting breakers. Save time & money!
