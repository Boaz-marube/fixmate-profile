OUR JOURNEY: From Idea To Impact

FixMate began with a flooded apartment, failed handyman calls, and a midnight crisis that exposed Nairobi’s broken repair system. In 2025, co-founders Boaz Marube and Brian Mwangi turned frustration into purpose—building a platform to restore trust in Africa’s informal trades. From coding during blackouts to onboarding vetted pros, FixMate has resolved over 6,491 urban emergencies, ensured fair pay for handymen, and brought peace of mind to countless renters. More than an app, it’s a movement proving that in cities where pipes burst and lights flicker, reliable help can be just a tap away.
